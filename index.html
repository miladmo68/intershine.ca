FAQ

Common questions, clear answers.

Everything you need to know before booking your first clean. Don't see your question? Ask us directly.

How quickly will I get a quote? +

We typically reply within a few hours and always within 24 hours of your request — often the same day. Send us a message any time and we'll respond as soon as we're able.

What areas of the GTA do you serve? +

We serve Toronto and the entire Greater Toronto Area, including Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, Oakville, Burlington, Etobicoke, North York, and Scarborough. If you're nearby and not sure, just ask.

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment? +

Yes — our team arrives fully equipped with the products and tools needed for the job. If you'd prefer we use specific products you provide, just mention it in your booking and we're happy to accommodate.

Can I book a one-time cleaning, or only recurring service? +

Both. We offer one-time cleans (great for moves, renovations, and special occasions) as well as weekly, biweekly, and monthly recurring service — whichever fits your space and routine.

How is pricing calculated? +

Pricing depends on the size of your space, the type of cleaning, and how often you book. After you send a request, we'll reply with a clear, itemized quote — no hidden fees, no surprises.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning? +

Not at all. Many clients give us access instructions and head out for the day. We'll secure your home when we leave and confirm completion so you know everything's ready when you return.
